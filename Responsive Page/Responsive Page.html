Freitag Redesign

Inspired by the production process of Freitag, I extracted three important tools for production, Knives, scissors, and sewing machines as the main elements for the rebranding. I intend to utilize the Freitag's production method and DIY activities as the main features of the brand for the redesign.

About Freitag

Freitag is a Swiss sustainable fashion brand founded in 1993 by brothers Markus and Daniel Freitag. The brand is known for its commitment to recycling and upcycling, primarily using discarded truck tarpaulins, old seat belts, and bicycle inner tubes to create a variety of unique bags and accessories.
